[[Calculus and Vectors]]

1. Find the equation of a line with slope $+5$ and passing through the point 					$(-2,4)$
	
	 $m=5$ point $(2,4)$\
	The **General Equation** of a line

	$$y=m(x-x_1)+y_1$$\
	$$y=5(x-(-2))+4$$
	$$y=5(x+2)+4$$
	$$y=5x+10+4$$
	$$y=5x+14$$
		
	The equation of the line is $y=5x+14$
